Entity Relationship Diagram

Entity-Relationship Diagrams (ERDs) are visual blueprints for databases. They depict entities (like customers or products) as boxes, their characteristics (attributes) as circles, and the connections between entities (relationships) as lines. ERDs help designers create logical, well-organized databases, facilitating clear communication and streamlining the development process.

Types of AI

This infographic explains the different types of artificial intelligence (AI) based on their capabilities. Artificial Narrow Intelligence (ANI) is designed for specific tasks, like spam filters or chess-playing computers. Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), still hypothetical, would match human intelligence. Artificial Super Intelligence (ASI) would surpass human intellect and is a subject of debate regarding its potential. The infographic uses concentric circles to illustrate these concepts, with ANI in the center, AGI in the middle, and ASI on the outer rim.

Data Science Lifecycle

  • Business Understanding: Defining the problem the project aims to solve.
  • Data Mining: Gathering relevant data from various sources.
  • Data Cleaning: Fixing errors, handling missing values, and formatting data for analysis.
  • Data Exploration: Visualizing and summarizing data to uncover patterns and insights.
  • Feature Engineering: Transforming or creating new features to improve model performance.
  • Predictive Modeling: Developing machine learning models to predict outcomes or classify data.
  • Data Visualization: Communicating findings through charts, graphs, and other visual representations.
